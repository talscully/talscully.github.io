Mal (Adult Green Dragon)

Huge Dragon (Prismatic), Typically Neutral

  • Armor Class 18 (Natural Armor)

  • Hit Points 237 (19d12 + 114)

  • Speed 40 ft., climb 40 ft., fly 80 ft., swim 40 ft.

Str25 (+7)
Dex10 (+0)
Con23 (+6)
Int14 (+2)
Wis17 (+3)
Cha19 (+4)

  • Saving Throws DEX +5, CON +11, WIS +8, CHA +9

  • Skills History +7, Nature +7, Perception +13, Stealth +5, Survival +8

  • Damage Resistances bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks

  • Damage Immunities poison

  • Senses blindsight 60 ft., darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 23

  • Languages Common, Draconic

  • Challenge 16 (15,000 XP)   Proficiency Bonus +5

Traits

Innate Spellcasting. Mal's innate spellcasting ability is Charisma. Mal can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

At-will: druidcraft

1/day each: hallucinatory terrain, invisibility, plant growth, speak with plants

Legendary Resistance (3/Day). If Mal fails a saving throw, she can choose to succeed instead.

Magic Resistance. Mal has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

Magic Weapons. Mal’s weapon attacks are magical.

Prismatic Weakness (Replaces Vulnerability to Acid Damage). If Mal is the target of a passwall spell or another spell of equal or greater level that can open a portal on a solid surface, the spell's normal effects do not happen. Instead, the spellcaster must make a DC 19 ability check using its spellcasting ability, adding the level of the spell to its roll. On a success, Mal temporarily loses the following features: Innate Spellcasting, Magic Resistance, Variable Radiance, and Wall of Prismatic Green. After 10 minutes, the features are restored, and this weakness cannot be triggered again for 1 hour.

Actions

Multiattack. Mal can use her Frightful Presence. She then makes three attacks: one with her bite and two with her claws.

Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (2d10 + 7) piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) poison damage.

Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d6 + 7) slashing damage.

Tail. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (1d8 + 7) bludgeoning damage.

Frightful Presence. Each creature of Mal's choice that is within 120 feet of Mal and aware of her must succeed on a DC 17 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a creature's saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the creature is immune to Mal's Frightful Presence for the next 24 hours.

Breath Weapons (Recharge 5–6). Mal uses one of the following breath weapons.

Poison Breath. Mal exhales poisonous gas in a 60-foot cone. Each creature in that area must make a DC 19 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the creature takes 56 (16d6) poison damage and is poisoned until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage and isn't poisoned.

Sleep Breath. Green rays of magical light flash from Mal's mouth in a 60-foot cone. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw or fall unconscious indefinitely. While asleep, the creature ceases aging and doesn't require food or drink. The creature wakes up if it takes damage or if another creature uses an action to shake it awake.

Wall of Prismatic Green (1/day). Mal casts prismatic wall (spell save DC 17), but the wall just has its green layer. The wall's bright and dim light each have a range of 60 feet instead of 100 feet, and its light isn't blinding.

If Mal casts this spell with a casting time of 10 minutes instead of 1 action, the wall remains in place until it's destroyed or Mal uses this feature again.

Bonus Actions

Variable Radiance. Mal chooses a radius up to 30 feet and glows green, shedding bright light in the chosen radius and dim light for an additional distance equal to the chosen radius. Mal stops glowing if she dies or chooses to stop as a bonus action.

Legendary Actions

Mal can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Mal regains spent legendary actions at the start of her turn.

Detect. Mal makes a Wisdom (Perception) check.

Tail Attack. Mal makes a tail attack.

Wing Attack (Costs 2 Actions). Mal beats her wings. Each creature within 10 feet of Mal must succeed on a DC 20 Dexterity saving throw or take 14 (2d6 + 7) bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone. Mal can then fly up to half her flying speed.

Lair Actions

On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), Mal takes a lair action to cause one of the following effects. Mal can’t use the same effect two rounds in a row.

Catch Breath. Mal rolls a d6. On a 6, it recharges its breath weapon action.

Lingering Breath. Any creature that took damage from Mal's breath weapon on her previous turn immediately takes 10 (3d6) damage of that type, as the breath weapon’s energy lingers and clings to its targets.

Grasping Plants. The dragon causes roots and vines to erupt in a 20-foot radius centered on a point on the ground that the dragon can see within 120 feet of it. That area becomes difficult terrain, and each creature there must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be restrained by the roots and vines. A creature can be freed if it or another creature takes an action to make a DC 15 Strength check and succeeds. The roots and vines wilt away when the dragon uses this lair action again or when the dragon dies.
