Hi! I'm Tal, a PhD candidate in the Harvard Systems Biology program, advised by Dr. Allon Klein. I am studying the evolution of red blood cells, for which I work with Ciona robusta, a tunicate which is closely related to vertebrates but lacks red blood cells. Before grad school, I went to MIT for undergrad, where I studied physics and theater. Outside the lab, I enjoy flute, Dungeons & Dragons, and bioethics.
